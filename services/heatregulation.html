Home automation Heating and Regulation

Common sense would say, "you have to heat only when you need it". Heating regulation by a home automation server

Our know-how in heating and regulation

A gas boiler without regulation is not what is best in terms of energy savings

That is why the use of a home automation server is advisable to regulate the temperature of a house while making the production of heat work only when it is necessary. It is not essential for example to maintain a comfortable temperature if you are not at home all day. Heating an apartment or a house that has the chance to have large windows facing south on a day of bright sun is also not necessarily necessary. It is for these reasons that a home automation server is in place to control the heat production of the home. A home automation server, contact modules shutters and windows to control the opening and internal and external temperature probes. to control the boiler. By turning off the heating when the ideal temperature is reached the apartment is not overheated when there is a beautiful sun coming through the windows. By lowering the temperature at night and back in the morning before waking up, savings are made. By lowering the temperature during the day when you are at work and going up before the usual time of return, savings are made. In case of cold sensation (and yes, not easy to find the best temperature right away) it was easy to increase or decrease the ideal temperature that had to reach the apartment via the smartphone..
